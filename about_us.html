About Us

Polar Bears is an organization that was created in 2021 with the purpose of focusing mainly on the safety of the habitat and life of polar bears. We are a recent organization due to the time we have been creating this project, but we already have a big supported platform that donates every year for this cause.

We use our best resources to keep saving polar bears from pollution, dangerous situations, and affected habitats, indeed it is very important for us to create awareness to people that affects them due to their actions.
