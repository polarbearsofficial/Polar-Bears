Polar Bears

Basic Information

  • Common Name: Polar Bear
  • Scientific Name: Ursus maritimus
  • Type: Mammal
  • Diet: Carnivore
  • Average Life Span: 25 to 30 years
  • Weight: Males: 410-720kg, females: 453.6kg
  • Size: Head and body: 2.2-2.4 meters, tail: 7.62-12.7cm.
  • Status: Vulnerable
  • Population: Unknown








(Beaufort, 2007)
