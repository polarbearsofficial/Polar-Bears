How to help?

Donating to:

  • Polar Bears International
  • WWF Gifts

Ways to help without donating:

  1. Eat less meat and dairy
  2. Cut back on flying
  3. Leave the car at home
  4. Respect and protect green spaces
  5. Cut consumption and waste

These ways of aid can help fight climate change, thus helping their environment.
